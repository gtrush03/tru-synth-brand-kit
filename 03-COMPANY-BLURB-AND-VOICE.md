## Supporting Context

### Tagline (ad series)
> **Your agents don't clock out. TRU Synth.**

### Product Surfaces (what the design system has to cover)

- **Dashboards:** Institutions (ALPHA / private capital), Governments (COMMAND / national ops), Enterprise (VELOCITY / commercial scale), Assets (The Foundry)
- **Simulations:** Synthesize Alpha · Mobilize Command · Scale Velocity

### Brand Voice Samples (from Production Bible + copy_dump)

- "Fluid, reactive interfaces designed for millions of concurrent users."
- "The vibe is aspirational: this is the life agents give you."

### Visual Rules (non-negotiables)
1. Black backgrounds, always. The brand is nocturnal.
2. No gradients as primary. No neon. No "tech-startup blue."
3. Gold (`#928466`) as accent only — never flood.
4. Logo, motion, and headline beat are consistent across every surface.
5. Two-line headline: faded line 1 (`text-white/60`) + solid line 2 (`text-white`).

### Sonic Identity (reference)
- Alarm cue: iPhone "Radar" ringtone — signature across all ads.
- End-card sting: 1s silence → low-frequency bass tone as logo appears.
- Music: high-energy tracks that contrast with on-screen action.

### Competitive Position
- **Moat:** cinematic marketing × working AI product.
- **Audience:** solo founders, SMBs, enterprises, developers, "vibe coders."
- **Strategy:** sell finished agents to business owners through media — not infrastructure to developers.
